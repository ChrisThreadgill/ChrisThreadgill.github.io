Boat BnB

Boat BnB is a clone of Boatsetter with an emphasis on a pixel-perfect design. This application utilizes React as its front-end framework and Redux for its front-end state management.
With Postgresql as its RBDMS paired with an Express backend and Sequelize as its ORM framework, Boat BnB is fast, reliable, and scalable.
